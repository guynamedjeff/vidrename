TL;DR: Copy your directory path and paste it in the form to retitle your media files.

This program will take a directory path from explorer and run that through a Python-based pattern recognition process. It will recognize the file extensions of your choosing, and it will appropriately retitle all files that contain those extensions.

VidRename is specifically designed to identify TV episodes and Movies. The end goal is to provide unification of filenames for the sake of readability and organization.
